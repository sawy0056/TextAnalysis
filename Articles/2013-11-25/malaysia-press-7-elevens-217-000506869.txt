Securities Commission Malaysia has rejected Vincent Tan's 700 million ringgit ($217.70 million) IPO of Seven Convenience Bhd, which runs the 7-Eleven outlets nationwide, said a source.The IPO was scheduled before the end of the year, according to the source. The reason for the rejection is not known.NOTE: Reuters has not verified this story and does not vouch for its accuracy.($1 = 3.2155 ringgit)
