OTTAWA, ONTARIO--(Marketwired - May 3, 2014) -Product photos are available at http://bit.ly/1rOmGyoHong Dao Business Development Ltd. is recalling Hankow brand Han Kow Style Noodle from the marketplace because it may contain peanut which is not declared on the label. People with an allergy to peanut should not consume the recalled product described below.The following product has been sold in Alberta, British Columbia, Ontario, and Quebec.Recalled product
