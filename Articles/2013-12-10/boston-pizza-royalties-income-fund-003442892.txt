VANCOUVER, BRITISH COLUMBIA--(Marketwired - Dec 9, 2013) -
Boston Pizza Royalties Income Fund (the "Fund") (TSX:BPF.UN) announced today a cash distribution to unitholders of 10.2 cents per unit for November 2013. The distribution will be payable to unitholders of record at the close of business on December 21, 2013 and will be paid on December 31, 2013.The Fund periodically reviews distribution levels based on its policy of stable and sustainable distribution flow to unitholders.The trustees of the Fund have approved the contents of this news release.
