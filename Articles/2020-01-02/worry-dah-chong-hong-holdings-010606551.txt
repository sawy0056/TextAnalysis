While this analysis focuses on total compensation, it's worth noting the salary is lower, valued at HK$5.3m. We looked at a group of companies with market capitalizations from HK$3.1b to HK$12b, and the median CEO total compensation was HK$3.7m.Thus we can conclude that Frank Lai receives more in total compensation than the median of a group of companies in the same market, and of similar size to Dah Chong Hong Holdings Limited. However, this doesn't necessarily mean the pay is too high.
