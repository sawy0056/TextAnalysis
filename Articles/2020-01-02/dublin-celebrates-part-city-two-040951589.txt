Country star Lisa McHugh and Ireland’s newest pop artist, Aimee, joined rock veterans Ash and platinum-selling Walking on Cars, who headlined the festival’s highlight event.And as 2019 drew to a close, crowds gathered along the quays for the fabulous Liffey Lights Midnight Moment.
