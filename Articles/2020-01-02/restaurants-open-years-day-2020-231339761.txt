But you will have to be patient.“With half of restaurants closed, and people itching to get out with their friends and family, your favorite local restaurant may be more busy than usual on New Year’s Day," said Brad Plothow, Womply vice president of corporate marketing and communications, who suggests checking for adjusted hours.New Year's Eve and New Year's Day hours: When Target, Bed Bath & Beyond, Best Buy, Walmart and grocery stores are openFreebie alert: Here's where to get free coffee, meal deals, drink specials on New Year’s Eve and New Year’s DayFast-food chains open New Year's DayCheck with your closest location before heading out.
