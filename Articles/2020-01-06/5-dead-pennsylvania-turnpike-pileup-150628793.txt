The bus went up an embankment and rolled over before being hit by two tractor trailers, Pennsylvania State Police spokesman Stephen Limani told reporters Sunday afternoon.Those two trucks were struck by another tractor trailer, and another passenger car was involved in the crash, Limani said, who described it as "extremely serious."The Westmoreland County Coroner's Office confirmed to FOX Business that five people had been killed. Fourteen National Transportation Safety Board investigators are on the scene, according to Limani.The bus was carrying foreigners, the majority of whom did not speak English, Limani said.
