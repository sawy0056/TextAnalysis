HANGMAN launches a suite of No Stud hangers to display TVs, soundbars, streaming devices and speakers in minutes. Launching atCES Booth 11129.SIMI VALLEY, Calif., Jan. 5, 2020 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- HANGMAN Products, known for innovative wall hanging solutions, will be showcasing 5 new products at CES 2020 in Las Vegas. HANGMAN has always been devoted to creating high quality, innovative products that are functional, easy to use, and engineered to the highest standards. Its newest addition of products uses that dedication and expertise to delve into smart home integration.With its award-winning and blockbuster No Stud Technology in mind, HANGMAN created a collection of shelves that seamlessly displays soundbars, streaming devices and speakers.HANGMAN's No Stud Smart Shelf™ installs with only tiny picture nails and a hammer, allowing the user to display their smart device virtually anywhere they like.The No Stud Sound Bar Shelf™, using the same technology, is the easiest and most efficient way for a user to integrate a soundbar into the home.The No Stud TV Hanger®, a hallmark product of the No Stud collection, has gotten an upgrade; it now supports televisions up to 65" TVs.The All Surface TV Hanger, HANGMAN's newest product outside of the No Stud family, can hang a television on virtually any surface. The hanger works on brick, concrete, wood and drywall with just one type of fastener and is UL rated for televisions from 32-80 inches in diameter.ProTrac™ Wall Organizer by HANGMAN is the ultimate wall organizing system. Created for novice and professionals alike, the ProTrac™ Wall Organizer can convert any wall into organizational space. The Protrac™ is great for craft rooms, game rooms, garages, or any empty wall.HANGMAN's new product collection will be available online at hangmanproducts.com beginning February 2020.About HANGMAN Products HANGMAN Products was founded in 1999 with the mission to reinvent picture hanging. Today, the Los Angeles based company has expanded its vision from picture hanging to consumer electronic accessories, anti-tip safety hardware, home decor and organization with over 30 patents and trademarks. The HANGMAN product line has received international design recognition and is available in the world's biggest retailers, as well as at hangmanproducts.com.
