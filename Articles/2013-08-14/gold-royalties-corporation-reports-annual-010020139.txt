CALGARY, ALBERTA--(Marketwired - Aug 13, 2013) -
Gold Royalties Corporation (TSX VENTURE:GRO) ("Gold Royalties" or "the Company")) is pleased to report that its shareholders approved all director nominations and matters presented at the Company's Annual and Special Meeting of Shareholders held at Calgary, Alberta on August 13, 2013."We appreciate shareholder support in the election of the Board of Directors and approval of the proposed resolutions," stated Ryan Kalt, President and Chief Executive Officer of Gold Royalties.About Gold Royalties CorporationGold Royalties Corporation is a publicly traded, growth-orientated royalty business that acquires and holds mining royalty assets for investment purposes.
