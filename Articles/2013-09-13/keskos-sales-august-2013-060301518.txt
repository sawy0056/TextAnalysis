KESKO CORPORATION STOCK EXCHANGE RELEASE 13.09.2013 AT 09.00 1(2)The Kesko Group`s sales, excluding VAT, in August 2013 totalled €825.3 million, down 6.5%.In Kesko`s food trade, sales in August were €378.4 million, a decrease of 3.1% compared to the previous year. In August, the number of delivery days was one less than in the previous year.In the home and speciality goods trade, sales in August were €122.9 million, a decrease of 12.8%. Sales performance was particularly impacted by the decrease in department store sales, as well as network changes in Musta Pörssi and Intersport Russia. Sales were up in Finland in Intersport and Budget Sport.In the building and home improvement trade, sales in August were €249.8 million, a decrease of 9.0%. Excluding the impact of retailer changes in Norway, the sales decreased by 4.0%. Sales in Finland were down 4.2%. Sales abroad decreased by 12.3% in terms of euros and by 8.1% in terms of local currencies. In August, the number of delivery days was one less than in the previous year.In the car and machinery trade, sales in August were €88.5 million, a decrease of 4.0%. VV-Auto`s sales in August were €54.4 million, down 8.0%. Konekesko`s sales in August were €34.1 million, an increase of 2.6% from the previous year.Kesko Group sales in euros, excluding VAT, in August 2013:
