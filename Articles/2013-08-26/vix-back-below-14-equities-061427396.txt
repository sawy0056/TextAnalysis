Major equity indexes saw back-to-back positive sessions at the end of last week, pushing the CBOE Volatility index below the 14 level. The S&P 500 gained 6.54 points to close at 1663.50. It surged at the end of the day, closing just off the session's high. It has resistance at 1680, with support at 1640 and then 1600. The Nasdaq 100 added 22.45 points to finish at 3124.27. It has resistance is at 3149 and first support at 3060. The Russell 2000 climbed 2.05 points to finish at 1038.24. It has resistance at 1055 and support at 1015. The VIX was down 0.78 points, or 5.28 percent, to 13.98--its first close below 14 since Aug. 14. The VIX futures followed lower, with the September contracts falling 0.25 points to 15.20 and October futures off down 0.15 points to close at 16.25.
