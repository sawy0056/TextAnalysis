(NASDAQ: AAPL). The South Korean tech giant started taking pre-orders for its $1,300 Galaxy 5G phone in May 2019, while Apple is expected to release its first 5G iPhones in the second half of 2020.Samsung has also benefited from the recent ban on Huawei.
