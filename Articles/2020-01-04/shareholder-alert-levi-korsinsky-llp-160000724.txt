There is no cost or obligation to you.The Southwest Georgia merger investigation concerns whether the Board of Southwest Georgia breached their fiduciary duties to stockholders by failing to adequately shop the Company before agreeing to enter into this transaction and whether First Bancshares is underpaying for Southwest Georgia shares, thus unlawfully harming Southwest Georgia shareholders.Levi & Korsinsky is a national firm with offices in New York, Connecticut, California, and Washington D.C.
