Announces Sale of Its Two MR1 Product TankersGlobeNewswireJanuary 3, 2020ReblogShareTweetShareATHENS, Greece, Jan. 03, 2020 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- TOP Ships Inc. (the “Company”), an international owner and operator of modern, fuel efficient "ECO" tanker vessels, announced today that it has sold to unaffiliated third parties its two MR1 Product Tankers, the M/T Eco Fleet and the M/T Eco Revolution (each weighing 39,000 tons).
