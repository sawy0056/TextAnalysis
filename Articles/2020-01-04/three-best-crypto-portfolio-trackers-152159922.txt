It allows users to input an array of cryptocurrencies alongside the price it was purchased for and/or sold at.The appealing user interface, coupled with it being used by several leading influencers, saw Blockfolio become one of the top downloaded cryptocurrency apps in 2017.CoinStatsView photosCoinStats seamlessly integrates cryptocurrency market data, financial news and portfolio tracking services.Unlike other portfolio providers CoinStats feeds directly into a variety of exchanges, giving users the chance to see the latest bid and ask price on respective exchanges as well as the volume and open interest.It also has the capability to sync wallets and exchanges to the app, which allows users to track the real-time value of their portfolio instantly.DeltaView photosDelta was another popular portfolio tracker from 2017.
