In Latvia, SIA Delfi Entertainment will be dissolved. The companies to be dissolved are currently dormant. In Estonia, new wholly-owned subsidiary OÜ Ekspress Finants will be set up through a spin-off from AS Printall and the group’s financial unit will be combined with it.
