Model 3 production made up nearly 87,000 units of the Q4 figures. Tesla shares were up about 0.3% amid the production/deliveries news.Apple (AAPL) - Analysts at Bank Of America and RBC raised their price targets on Apple to $330 Friday morning. With Apple shares last at about $298, the new price targets represented potential price appreciation of about 11%.Yuma Energy (YUMA) - Shares were up nearly 80% pre market amid crude oil which was up more than 3.5% to over the $63/barrel level. Traders have targeted this name previously given volatility in oil markets; the company has a float of just over 1 million shares, making it susceptible to increased volatility.Anixter (AXE) - WESCO (WCC) raised its offer for the company from $93.50 to $97/share.
