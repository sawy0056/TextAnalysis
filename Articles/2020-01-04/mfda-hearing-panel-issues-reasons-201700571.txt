In particular, the Respondent:has paid a fine of $11,000 ; has paid costs of $2,500 ; and shall in the future comply with MFDA Rule 2.1.1.A copy of the Reasons for Decision is available on the MFDA website at www.mfda.ca. During the period described in the Reasons for Decision, the Respondent carried on business in the Vernon, British Columbia area.The MFDA is the self-regulatory organization for Canadian mutual fund dealers, regulating the operations, standards of practice and business conduct of its Members and their approximately 81,000 Approved Persons with a mandate to protect investors and the public interest.
