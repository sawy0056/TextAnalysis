An unspecified amount of money damages is requested due to the alleged fault of Diamond Services Corporation and its crane operator."This is a legitimate case, I would never file a lawsuit like this otherwise," said Cormier.The claimant is represented by Lafayette maritime attorney William Gee, who said, "We just want the best for Ben.
