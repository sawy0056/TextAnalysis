Cui Xiangxiu, the Chairman of the Reith Group, Jia Fan, the Founder and CEO of Bee +, representatives of the two enterprises, and nearly 500 guests attended the opening ceremony.View photoslogoMore View photosRibbon-cutting ceremonyMoreA benchmark project for high-end spaces in Bao'an, Shenzhen, facilitating the integrated development of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay AreaBased in Qianhai, a state-built high ground for financial innovation, and tasked with the mission of connecting Hong Kong, Macao and international finance, Bao'an has always been the focus of development and spatial planning in the major city of Shenzhen. Bee +, the leading space operator in China for building space products, and the Reith Group, a large-scale comprehensive real estate group with the most promising growth in shared resources, with their mutually complementary advantages, have joined together to build the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space in Bao'an, Shenzhen. This space will be the future Manhattan with the booming business in China, and is a benchmark project for high-end spaces, integrating business services, high-net-worth communities, butler services, and other supporting services.The "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space is located on the 1st and the 3rd-5th floors of the Jiuyue Yaxuan Commercial Podium Building in Bao'an, Shenzhen, covering nearly 10,000m2. It offers 1,600 seats, 157 independent working spaces, and 7 standard conference rooms for its co-working customers and co-working elites. As one of the representative projects for high-end co-working spaces in Bao'an, Shenzhen, the grand launch of the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space is attracting high-quality, innovative, and entrepreneurial enterprises in Shenzhen and GBA, providing them with unprecedentedly strong resource networking and a future-oriented, high-end working experience, while also injecting strong momentum into the integrated development of Qianhai in Shenzhen and the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area.Story continuesInnovating to create the new form of an "exterior scene moving inside", revolutionizing the conception of futuristic co-working spacesAs the most forward-looking, high-end working service space Bao'an, Shenzhen, the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space was independently designed by the Bee + Design Institute. The designers drew inspiration for the spatial design from European and American towns, and adopted the innovative design concept of an "exterior scene moving inside", in which the exterior scene on the street is moved into the interior space, thus maximizing the interactive integration of people and scenery, fully enlarging the spatial perception on the psychological level of the customers, and revolutionizing a new benchmark for the innovative design of co-working spaces in the industry.View photosLive view of the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working spaceMoreAdhering to Bee+'s product space building concept of "Bring Life into Work", the designers of Bee+ constantly revolutionize and innovate in the building of space products, and continue to make new upgrades while retaining the Bee+ classic design concept and style. The three functional areas were designed in the style of a "cross-floor void structure": the Bee store (self-service sales area), the Bee Park (double-deck slide), and Central Street. The dramatic design elements and ingenious arrangements enable all those experiencing the space to immerse themselves in the unique atmosphere of spare time social life in European and American towns, to release tension and stress, and to inspire their infinite creativity and insight.As another major flagship project for Bee+ in their co-working sector of customization for large and medium-sized enterprises, Bee+ provides one-stop space customization services for the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space, from the design and construction, to the later operation management. The design of the work area breaks down the interface style design of traditional spaces through the use of transparent curved glass, enhancing social interaction while preserving privacy. The open area and independent offices are designed to meet the working needs of one to a hundred enterprises of different scales. The space is fully equipped with meeting rooms, brainstorming rooms, document printing areas, automatic lifting tables and chairs, a lunch break cabin, a beverage corridor, front desk administrative services, and a complete array of other supporting facilities and services for working, thus truly realizing entry at any time. The "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space has built a high-end service platform to enable the elite creators in this space to help their enterprises take off.The grand opening ceremony was magnificent and the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space officially openedOn the day of the opening ceremony a grand tour of American style was held in the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space, with six immersive and interactive experience areas were set up, including cowgirls, a jazz band, close-up magic, hand graffiti, and others. The novel and interesting theme games and the wonderful performance of the actors allowed the guests to feel the unique charm of the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space with the enthusiastic atmosphere of American style.At the opening ceremony, Jia Fan, the Founder and CEO of Bee+, Li Shijun, the head of Reith Property Services, Gao Lei, the head of the Planning Center at Reith Group, Cai Jingqian, the head of the Marketing Center at Reith Group, Liang Minyu, a Vice President of Bee+, and Wang Bin, also a Vice President of Bee+, jointly held the grand ribbon-cutting ceremony, which announced the official opening of the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space.Mr. Cai Jingqian, the head of the Marketing Center at Reith Group, said in his speech that Reith Group, which is a large-scale comprehensive company integrating real estate development, equity investment, asset operation, and property management, cooperated with Bee+ to realize the more diversified development of the Reith Group. He also affirmed that Bee+ has efficient operational capacity in building high-quality spaces, and expressed his great expectations for the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space in providing more flexible leasing methods for its settled enterprises.Wang Bin, a Vice President of Bee+, said in his speech that tonight's perfect launch of the "Reith & BEEPLUS" co-working space was inseparable from the joint efforts of Reith Group and their Bee+ partners.
