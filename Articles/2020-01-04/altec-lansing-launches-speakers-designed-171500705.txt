The latest Altec Lansing party speakers include new durable, rugged and portable Everything-Proof Bluetooth Speakers and new At-Home speaker models, designed to fit in perfectly with any home theater or entertainment system. All speakers in the party line feature high-quality sound and dynamic LED lights that beat, strobe and flash to the music."We understand that today's consumers aren't just looking for high-quality audio products. They're searching for speakers with unique features and added value that fit their lifestyle," said Joe Campos, VP Sales and Marketing at Altec Lansing. "With our latest party speakers, consumers will enjoy the quality audio they have come to know from Altec Lansing coupled with a feature set that will be sure to be a hit at their next gathering."Take the party with you wherever you go with Altec Lansing Everything-Proof Bluetooth Party Speakers. These speakers feature a rugged design and sturdy exterior with an IP67 rating, making them waterproof, snowproof, dirtproof and dustproof. Additionally, each party speaker includes a unique feature designed to help with party planning, whether that be LED light modes that beat-sync to your music or included cup holders to hold your drinks! Designed to seamlessly fit into any home theater or entertainment system, these speakers will fill your room with immersive stereo sound.
