Announces December 2019 Investment Fund Sales and Total Assets Under ManagementCNW GroupJanuary 3, 2020ReblogShareTweetShareWINNIPEG , Jan. 3, 2020 /CNW/ - IGM Financial Inc. (IGM) (IGM.TO) today reported preliminary total investment fund net new money of $110.2 million as shown in Table 1. Investment fund assets under management were $161.8 billion at December 31, 2019 , compared with $161.4 billion at November 30, 2019 and $143.3 billion at December 31, 2018 . Total assets under management were $166.8 billion at December 31, 2019 , compared with $166.4 billion at November 30, 2019 and $149.1 billion at December 31, 2018 . IGM Financial Inc. is one of Canada's leading diversified wealth and asset management companies with approximately $167 billion in total assets under management.
