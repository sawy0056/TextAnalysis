The infographic breaks down the different aspects and process that goes into how communities regulate vacation rental properties.According to LODGINGRevs, "Having regulations is important because it cuts down on nuisance problems, limits occupancy, and enforces zoning regulations. By having clear guidelines, both the property owners and renters are offered protection.
