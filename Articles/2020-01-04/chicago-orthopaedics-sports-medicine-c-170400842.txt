Joins Illinois Bone & Joint InstitutePR NewswireJanuary 3, 2020ReblogShareTweetShareDES PLAINES, Ill., Jan. 3, 2020 /PRNewswire/ -- Illinois Bone & Joint Institute (IBJI), one of the largest independent, physician-owned orthopedic practices in the United States, is happy to announce today that Chicago Orthopaedics & Sports Medicine, S.C. (COSM) will be joining our practice as of January 1st, 2020. COSM has a physician office and physical therapy located at 3000 N. Halsted St., Suite 525, Chicago, IL 60657 and an additional physician office located at 331 W. Surf St., Suite 6400, Chicago, IL 60657.View photosIllinois Bone & Joint Institute welcomes Chicago Orthopaedics & Sports Medicine, S.C. as of January 1st, 2020.MoreThrough our joined efforts, COSM patients will benefit from the expanded resources and expertise provided through IBJI's large network of physicians, rehabilitation, and imaging services. "We are grateful to be able to provide our patients with the same great comprehensive and personalized care they have come to expect, now under our new IBJI brand," says Dr. David Guelich, President, Chicago Orthopaedics & Sports Medicine, S.C.IBJI has a long history of offering patients collaborative, integrated orthopedic care including OrthoAccess walk-in clinics, MRI and outpatient physical and occupational therapy services with convenient locations in the downtown, Avondale/Logan Square and Sauganash communities.If you have any questions, please call the COSM office at (773) 433-3130 or visit ibji.com.About Illinois Bone & Joint InstituteIllinois Bone & Joint Institute, is one of the largest independent physician-owned orthopedic practices in the U.S. IBJI is dedicated to providing accessible, compassionate, and comprehensive care to your family for every orthopedic specialty with leading-edge technology and innovative treatment options.
