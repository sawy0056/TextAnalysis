(the “Fund”) (KMF) today provided a summary unaudited statement of assets and liabilities and announced its net asset value and asset coverage ratios under the Investment Company Act of 1940 (the “1940 Act”) as of December 31, 2019. As of December 31, 2019, the Fund’s net assets were $582 million and its net asset value per share was $12.32.  As of December 31, 2019, the Fund’s asset coverage ratio under the 1940 Act with respect to senior securities representing indebtedness was 427% and the Fund’s asset coverage ratio under the 1940 Act with respect to total leverage (debt and preferred stock) was 311%.
