NLJC strives to recognize the individuals that students look to as leaders in social etiquette and all-encompassing manners.            The National League of Junior Cotillions is headquartered in Charlotte, NC and has licensed chapters throughout the United States. It hosts the popular cotillion website and etiquette blog (www.nljc.com).             We are currently looking for Directors to expand into new territories available across the country.  Each chapter is an independent franchise operated by a local Director.  Our next New Director Training will take place in February of 2020 in Charlotte, NC.
