(CSV) ("Carriage Services" or the "Company") today announced that it closed the acquisition of Oakmont Memorial Park and Mortuary in Lafayette, California from StoneMor Partners.  The business was founded in 1956 and serves the East Bay Area including Oakland and many communities in Contra Costa County.Mel Payne stated, “Since October, we have now acquired four remarkable 'Best in Class' businesses that are wonderful additions to our current portfolio.  We look forward to sharing updates on these acquisitions as well as our full year 2019 financial performance when we issue our year-end earnings release in February.”About Carriage ServicesCarriage Services is a leading provider of deathcare services and merchandise in the United States.
