As of December 31, 2019, the Company’s asset coverage ratio under the 1940 Act with respect to senior securities representing indebtedness was 424% and the Company’s asset coverage ratio under the 1940 Act with respect to total leverage (debt and preferred stock) was 291%.        Kayne Anderson MLP/Midstream Investment Company
