Shares of Waddell & Reed Financial Inc. (WDR) have been on the rise since this mutual fund asset manager announced better-than-expected third-quarter results in late October, including a hike in its quarterly cash dividend. Given its strong fundamentals, the valuation looks reasonable.Waddell & Reed has been continuously outperforming the S&P 500 and 200 day moving average over the last three months.
