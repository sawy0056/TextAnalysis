OTTAWA, ONTARIO--(Marketwired - Jul 13, 2013) -
The Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA) and Mom's Ukrainian Homestyle Foods are warning people with allergies to milk, soy, or mustard not to consume the Mom's Ukrainian Homestyle brand products described below.
