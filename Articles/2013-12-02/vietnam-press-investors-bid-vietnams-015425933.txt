Seven foreign companies applied to invest in Vietnam's first public-private partnership at the bidding registration on Friday, the Dau Tu (Investment) newspaper reports. The foreign firms include Egis Projects SA & Consortium of IJM Corp Berhad and IL & FS Transportation Network.The $757 million expressway project is funded by the Vietnamese government and two private investors. Vietnam's Bitexco Group was appointed as the first investor, the report said.----NOTE: Reuters has not verified this story and does not vouch for its accuracy.
