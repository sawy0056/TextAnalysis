If you choose to take no action, you can remain an absent class member.According to the Complaint, the Company made false and misleading statements to the market. Forescout suffered from significant volatility related to large customer orders and poor execution on deals in the pipeline, especially in EMEA. These problems were likely to have a material impact on the Company's financial results. Based on these facts, the Company's public statements were false and materially misleading throughout the class period.
