(Reuters) - Royal Dutch Shell Plc (LSE:RDSA) said it was restarting a unit at its Scotford facility near Edmonton, Alberta, according to a message posted on a community information line on Saturday.There was no material impact to production associated with the activity, the company said.Shell runs a 100,000 barrel-per-day refinery in Scotford.(Reporting by Koustav Samanta in Bangalore; Editing by Gopakumar Warrier)
