VANCOUVER, BRITISH COLUMBIA--(Marketwired - Oct 23, 2013) -
Passport Potash Inc. ("Passport" or the "Company") (TSX VENTURE:PPI)(PPRTF) announces, further to our news releases on March 13, 2013 and October 21, 2013, that an amended Preliminary Economic Assessment, with a report date of October 17, 2013, has been filed on SEDAR.About the Holbrook Potash ProjectPassport Potash Inc. is a publicly traded corporation engaged in the exploration and development of advanced potash properties with its major focus on a previously explored potash property in Arizona. Passport has acquired a strategic position in the Holbrook Basin with land holdings encompassing over 127,000 acres.
