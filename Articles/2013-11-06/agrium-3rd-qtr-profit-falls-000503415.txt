Nov 5 (Reuters) - Canadian fertilizer company Agrium Inc reported 50 cents adjusted quarterly profit on Tuesday after financial markets closed.Net earnings for the third quarter fell to $76 million, or 52 cents per share, compared with $129 million, or 80 cents per share, a year ago, while revenue rose 1 percent to $2.87 billion.Adjusted earnings per share were $73 million, or 50 cents per share.
