CALGARY, ALBERTA--(Marketwired - Nov 5, 2013) -
Trican Well Service Ltd. (TCW.TO) is pleased to announce that its Board of Directors has declared a $C 0.15 dividend per share in accordance with the semi-annual dividend policy for holders of Trican Common Shares. The date of record will be December 31, 2013 and the date of payment will be January 14, 2014.Headquartered in Calgary, Alberta, Trican has operations in Canada, the United States, Russia, Kazakhstan, Australia and Norway. Trican provides a comprehensive array of specialized products, equipment and services that are used during the exploration and development of oil and gas reserves.
