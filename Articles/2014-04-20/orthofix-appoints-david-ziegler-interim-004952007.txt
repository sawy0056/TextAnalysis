Orthofix disclosed in a regulatory filing that on April 17, the Board of Directors of Orthofix International appointed David E. Ziegler to the position of Interim CFO effective immediately. The company’s prior CFO, Emily V. Buxton, notified the company on April 14 of her voluntary resignation of employment from the company effective as of May 14 and has ceased to serve as the company’s principal financial and accounting officer. The Board is currently conducting a search for a permanent CFO, and intends that Mr. Ziegler will serve as the company’s principal financial and accounting officer until such search is completed.
