MarkWest Energy Partners announced that the board of the General Partner of MarkWest Energy Partners declared a cash distribution of 86c per common unit for Q4, for an implied annual rate of $3.44 per common unit. The Q4 distribution represents an increase of 4c per common unit, or 4.9%, compared to 4Q12 distribution and an increase of 1c per common unit, or 1.2%, compared to the Q3 distribution. The Q4 distribution is payable February 14 to unitholders of record on February 6. The ex-dividend date is February 4.
