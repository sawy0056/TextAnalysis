View our latest analysis for iomart Group How Does Angus MacSween's Compensation Compare With Similar Sized Companies?At the time of writing, our data says that iomart Group plc has a market cap of UK£403m, and reported total annual CEO compensation of UK£618k for the year to March 2019. While we always look at total compensation first, we note that the salary component is less, at UK£359k. We looked at a group of companies with market capitalizations from UK£153m to UK£611m, and the median CEO total compensation was UK£665k.So Angus MacSween receives a similar amount to the median CEO pay, amongst the companies we looked at. Considering these factors I'd say performance has been pretty decent, though not amazing.
