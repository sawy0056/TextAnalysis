In Japan, many SMEs, which are at startup position and are not equipped for licensing the software are switching to SaaS based model as they are subscription based and cost effective.Growth in IT industry in the country coupled with increased demand for data analysis software, high speed and customized solutions are favoring the growth of SaaS market.
