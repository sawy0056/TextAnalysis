Equation Technologies provides business management solutions also known as ERP software for mid-sized companies. Equation Technologies unique approach to understanding business needs prior to offering carefully crafted ERP software selection and ERP software implementation recommendations set them apart from other ERP software partners and consultants.Equation Technologies was incorporated in 2001, when technology was changing, and companies were migrating from their outdated legacy systems. Since 2001, technology has advanced so much that Equation Technologies have added ERP software systems to their portfolio and have developed integrations with other systems, including many independent software vendors (ISVs) to offer solutions that best fit customer needs. These solutions include on-premise, SaaS (software as a service), as well as Equation Technologies own cloud hosting services.Erica Burles, Founder, serves as the President of Equation Technologies. She is an experienced Certified Public Accountant (CPA) with deep domain experience in accounting, review, and tax services.Erica leads her team to deliver ERP software implementations on time and on budget. Each ERP software implementation project requires a rigorous needs analysis and discovery to determine if there is a proper fit between the client, company, and the software package being considered. It is imperative to be honest and open with clients about the possibilities and limitations of each package. An optimal project plan and design are created before handing off the project to the consulting team. Equation Technologies Founder, Erica Burles was inducted to The National Association of Professional Women (NAPW) in 2016-2017 VIP Woman of the Year Circle. She is recognized with this prestigious distinction for leadership in business and consulting services. Erica is also an active contributor to Huffington Post and has been featured as a guest speaker on Sage Advice Podcast. Erica holds a Bachelor of Science in Accounting."We know that one single approach is not the right one for all businesses. We use our vast education and business experiences to strengthen our relationships with our clients to focus on their specific needs," says Erica Burles, Founder and President, Equation Technologies, Inc.About Equation Technologies Equation Technologies provides business management solutions for mid-sized companies.
