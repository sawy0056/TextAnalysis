(AP Photo/Henny Ray Abrams, File)MoreFive years after the financial crisis, people in the world's 10 biggest economies are still too spooked and distrustful to take chances with their money. Families continue to spend cautiously and have pulled hundreds of billions of dollars out of stocks.
