GBP/USD Technical Analysis – Prices advanced as expected after putting in a Bullish Engulfing candlestick pattern and clearing falling trend line resistance set from early May. A break above initial resistance at 1.5149, the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement, has exposed the 38.2% level at 1.5236. A further push above that aims for the 50% Fib at 1.5307.
