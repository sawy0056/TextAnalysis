I was honored, under David's leadership and encouragement, to become the first African American majority owner of an NBA basketball team, the Charlotte Bobcats. David's legacy will be that of a one-of-a-kind individual who nurtured and built a sports league that will leave an indelible and giant footprint in the global arena of sports competition and the unifying culture of sports in our lives."The RLJ Companies logo.
