The Maryland hotels are located throughout Frederick, Washington, Allegany and Howard Counties."This is an exciting time for our company," said Peter Plamondon, Jr., Co-President of Plamondon Hospitality Partners. "We're happy to be adding two additional Hilton properties to our portfolio, but also to be expanding our footprint in our home state of Maryland."The Homewood Suites by Hilton opened in September 2015 and the Home2 Suites by Hilton opened in June 2018. The purpose-built extended stay hotels have 117 rooms each and both feature on-site swimming pools, fitness centers and free parking. These hotels now represent the second and third acquisition by Plamondon Hospitality Partners in the last six months. They acquired the Fairfield by Marriott Cumberland in July 2019.Plamondon Hospitality Partners prides itself on its commitment to associates and providing them with the tools they need to succeed in both their personal and professional lives. The company recently launched a program in conjunction with the Literacy Council of Frederick County to provide English language classes to associates. They also offer driver's education courses and tuition reimbursement.Plamondon Hospitality Partners is a hotel management and business development company located in Frederick, Maryland.
