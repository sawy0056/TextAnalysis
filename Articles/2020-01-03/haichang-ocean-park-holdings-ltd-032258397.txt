We think total compensation is more important but we note that the CEO salary is lower, at CN¥1.5m. When we examined a selection of companies with market caps ranging from CN¥1.4b to CN¥5.6b, we found the median CEO total compensation was CN¥2.3m.So Xuguang Wang is paid around the average of the companies we looked at.
