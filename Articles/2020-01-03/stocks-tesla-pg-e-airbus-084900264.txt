That confirms Airbus’s dethronement of Boeing (NYSE:BA) as the world’s no. 1 maker of airplanes. Boeing had only delivered 345 planes in the 11 months through November. Its stock was up 0.7% in premarket.Altria (NYSE:MO) stock was up 0.5% after The Wall Street Journal reported that the Food and Drug Administration is likely to exempt menthol from the vaping flavors that it intends to ban, after research showed the measure would be unpopular in key states in an election year.Tesla (NASDAQ:TSLA) stock was up 2.2% after Canaccord Genuity upgraded its target price to $515 from $370, predicting a big acceleration in the electric vehicle revolution in the year ahead.
