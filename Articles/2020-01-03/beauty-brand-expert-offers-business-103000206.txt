Announced today, Angelo De Simone, founder of Nuovaluce Beauty, is using his expertise as a business industry thought leader to help new and established businesses position themselves for success."I founded Nuovaluce Beauty in 2017 after noticing a lack of sustainable and reliable at-home solutions for microcurrent technology," said De Simone, founder, Nuovaluce Beauty.
