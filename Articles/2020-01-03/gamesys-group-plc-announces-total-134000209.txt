There are no shares held in Treasury.Therefore, the total number of voting rights in the Company is 108,665,248.The above total number of voting rights may be used by shareholders as the denominator for the calculation by which they will determine if they are required to notify their interest in, or a change in their interest in the Company under the FCA's Disclosure Guidance and Transparency Rules.About Gamesys Group plcGamesys Group plc is the parent company of an online gaming group that provides entertainment to a global consumer base.
