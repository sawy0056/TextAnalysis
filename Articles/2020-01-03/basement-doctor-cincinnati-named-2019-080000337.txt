The awards show the practices that these organizations employ to generate a high level of trust among their employees, customers, and their communities.The Torch Awards for Marketplace Ethics is Greater Cincinnati's only ethics-centered awards program. For 19 years, local businesses and nonprofits have demonstrated their dedication to ethics by going through a process of self-evaluation and submitting to feedback from a panel of judges. This year, The Basement Doctor of Cincinnati was among 5 Cincinnati companies recognized as Torch recipients. They were honored at the Torch Awards Event Friday, October, 18 at the Sharonville Convention Center.The Basement Doctor has garnered numerous awards over the years. In addition to the Torch, the company is a multiple year winner of the Consumers' Choice Award among other accolades. "It's a privilege to honor a business like Basement Doctor," said Corrie McGlothlin, Vice President of BBB Center for Ethics. The Torch Awards sets exceptional organizations apart. We believe celebrating the best businesses makes our community better".Since its founding in 1987, The Basement Doctor of Cincinnati has provided solutions to home owners with foundation and basement problems in the Cincinnati Tri-State area. They provide complete basement waterproofing, foundation repair, crawl space encapsulation, and concrete leveling services."We feel it's an honor to be a part of the BBB, and the highest honor is to be able to win the Torch Award," said Bobby Miles, owner of The Basement Doctor. The company is thankful to those who helped throughout the Torch Award Process.About The Basement Doctor: As a member of the international Basement Systems Network, The Basement Doctor of Cincinnati has access to the best products and training in the industry. The company has patented and proven products that are designed to provide long-lasting solutions.
