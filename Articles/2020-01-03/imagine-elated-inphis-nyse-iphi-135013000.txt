While not every stock performs well, when investors win, they can win big. Don't believe it? Then look at the Inphi Corporation (NYSE:IPHI) share price. It's 306% higher than it was five years ago. This just goes to show the value creation that some businesses can achieve. Also pleasing for shareholders was the 18% gain in the last three months. But this could be related to the strong market, which is up 11% in the last three months. View our latest analysis for Inphi Inphi isn't currently profitable, so most analysts would look to revenue growth to get an idea of how fast the underlying business is growing. Shareholders of unprofitable companies usually expect strong revenue growth. Some companies are willing to postpone profitability to grow revenue faster, but in that case one does expect good top-line growth.In the last 5 years Inphi saw its revenue grow at 17% per year. That's well above most pre-profit companies. Arguably, this is well and truly reflected in the strong share price gain of 32%(per year) over the same period. It's never too late to start following a top notch stock like Inphi, since some long term winners go on winning for decades.
