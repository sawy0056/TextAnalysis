With this connector, there is no longer the need for teams to manually enter that Procore data into Projectmates. Contractors can pull RFIs and submittals to Projectmates as they happen. The integrated data exchange between the two platforms is designed to improve transparency and minimize double entry while maintaining data integrity."It is a great pleasure for us to release this connector. It's something that our clients have been asking for. It's always encouraging to know we're making our clients lives easier. Contractors can now pull their RFIs and submittals to Projectmates in an instant. Synching RFIs saves double entry and avoids costly errors. It's a tremendous benefit to both of our user bases." -Varsha Bhave, President and CTO of ProjectmatesAbout Projectmates- For 20 years, Projectmates has been pushing the envelope for construction management software. Projectmates is an intuitive and easy to use construction project management platform. From conception to closeout, Projectmates has focused on project owners and their partners.Our Construction Project and Program Management software is thoughtfully crafted to solve the construction industry's most pressing challenges from the owner's perspective.
