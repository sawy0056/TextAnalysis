Quilter
(LON:QLT) said it has completed the sale of its life assurance business QLA to ReAssure for 445 million pounds ($558 million), including accrued interest.The company said it intends to distribute the full net surplus proceeds of 375 million pounds to shareholders. Tullow Oil
(LON:TLW), whose shares were among the worst performing in the FTSE 250 in 2019. Tullow holds a 37.5% interest in the block.
