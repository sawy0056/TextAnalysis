CALGARY, ALBERTA--(Marketwired - July 26, 2013) - Prize Mining Corporation (TSX VENTURE:PRZ.H) (the "Corporation") announces that it has successfully received an order from the Court of Queen's Bench of Alberta extending the time in which it is permitted to hold its annual general and special meeting of shareholders (the "AGM") to a date no later than October 31, 2013.The Corporation previously obtained an order extending the time to no later than August 31, 2013. The Corporation sought a further extension because it is considering various transactions, which may require shareholder approval. With a view to minimizing costs, the Corporation plans to delay holding the AGM pending the successful negotiation of a transaction.
