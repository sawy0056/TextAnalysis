That makes EPS growth an attractive quality for any company. We can see that in the last three years SpareBank 1 SMN grew its EPS by 4.3% per year. That might not be particularly high growth, but it does show that per-share earnings are moving steadily in the right direction.Careful consideration of revenue growth and earnings before interest and taxation (EBIT) margins can help inform a view on the sustainability of the recent profit growth. I note that SpareBank 1 SMN's revenue from operations was lower than its revenue in the last twelve months, so that could distort my analysis of its margins. SpareBank 1 SMN maintained stable EBIT margins over the last year, all while growing revenue 11% to kr5.9b.
