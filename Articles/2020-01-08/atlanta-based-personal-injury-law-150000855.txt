All positions come with health insurance, dental insurance, vision insurance, 401k retirement plans and wellness programs."The two qualities we look for when adding members to our team are Positivity and Productivity," says Monge. "We want people that have a positive life view on the world and have shown they can produce results," adds Monge.Monge states, "We are looking to add 30 members to our team who have a passion for being a positive influence on the world and are accepting resumes from all backgrounds and experience levels in order to find the perfect candidates." The firm seeks candidates with a positive outlook on the world that have successfully overcome life challenges.The 30 new hires will join a company where positive culture is critical to delivering a good experience for innocent injury victims that have had their lives turned upside down by tragedy. Monge notes, "It's important that people feel valued, appreciated and important and that's true not only for our clients, but for members of our team. This is one of the reasons our team members nominated us as one of top places to work in Atlanta for 2019," declares Monge.To support the expanded staff Monge & Associates is also expanding their office spaces in its headquarters in Atlanta, as well as their offices in South Carolina and Tennessee.Candidates interested in applying for an open position can do so at https://www.becauseyouwanttowin.com/join-our-team/.About Monge & AssociatesMonge & Associates, "The Hammer," is a national recognized personal injury law firm that makes good things happen for other people by helping innocent injury victims hurt through the fault of another person. Primary practice areas include helping victims of drunk drivers, surgery cases, catastrophic injuries, car wrecks, truck collisions, motorcycle accidents, negligent security injuries, injuries happening at businesses, worker's compensation case and victims hurt by dangerous products.
