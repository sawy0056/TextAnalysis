USDC is the third stablecoin offering by Kraken, after Tether (USDT) and DAI.The supply of USDC has risen by almost 98% since Jan. 1, 2019 at over 518 million, according to The Block's research. Kraken has also established five trading pairs for USDC: bitcoin (BTC)/ USDC; ether (ETH)/USDC; USDC/ euro (EUR); USDC/ U.S. dollar (USD) and USDC/USDT.Notably, the USDC support is currently only available for Kraken, Kraken Pro and its over-the-counter trading platforms.
