The author's personal journey will inspire readers to be the leader of their own lives and to embrace their successes and adversities that contribute to who they are. Richard's story expresses that everything in life happens for a reason and to never give up on the values that identify who you are as an individual and as a leader."I wrote this book to provide a perspective on leading that is personal and, yet, applicable to every reader finding deeper meaning about their own journey," said Richard. "It was a negative experience with leadership that changed the trajectory of my life and brought new purpose. I felt blessed to have grown up with golf and to possess the wherewithal to be reflective about what it was teaching me about myself and the world."Richard benefited greatly from the talents and insights of others who believed in her success. Through her interactions and experiences, she was able to learn to bring the best of herself to those who needed her inspiration and passion. Richard's memoir will leave readers with an inspirational message that will help individuals find and understand their why."Trust: Understanding My Why" By Deb Richard ISBN: 978-1-9822-3443-0 (hardcover); 978-1-9822-3447-8 (eBook) Available through Amazon, Barnes & Noble and Burlap LeadersAbout the author Deb Richard is the CEO of Burlap Leaders and is a former professional golfer with twenty years of experience under her belt. She competed on the LPGA tour and captured six titles. Richard is the 1984 US Women's Amateur Champion, 1984 World Team Amateur Champion (individual and team USA) and in 1985 lead her University of Florida women's golf team to its first national championship. Richard grew up in Manhattan, KS and resides in Kansas City. Philanthropically, she has built three non-profits to positively impact the lives of others: Deb Richard Foundation, Soaring with Eagles Foundation, and Sports Academy Foundation.
