See our latest analysis for Hans Energy How Does Dong Yang's Compensation Compare With Similar Sized Companies?According to our data, Hans Energy Company Limited has a market capitalization of HK$1.1b, and paid its CEO total annual compensation worth HK$2.4m over the year to December 2018. While this analysis focuses on total compensation, it's worth noting the salary is lower, valued at HK$1.3m. We looked at a group of companies with market capitalizations under HK$1.6b, and the median CEO total compensation was HK$1.8m.It would therefore appear that Hans Energy Company Limited pays Dong Yang more than the median CEO remuneration at companies of a similar size, in the same market. However, this fact alone doesn't mean the remuneration is too high.
