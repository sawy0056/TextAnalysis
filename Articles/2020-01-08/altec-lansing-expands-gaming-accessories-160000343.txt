The range of accessories will not only include gaming headsets and other audio products with the quality, immersive sound consumers have come to know and expect from Altec Lansing, but will also include a wide range of mobile, PC and console gaming accessories designed to offer casual gamers and enthusiasts quality products they can trust at an exceptional value.The new range is designed and manufactured by Premier Accessory Group, who has the exclusive license to Altec Lansing gaming accessories in the U.S. and Canada. The licensing agreement includes gaming headsets, keyboards, mice, PC speakers, microphones, mobile, PC and console controllers and accessories, and AR / VR solutions."Throughout our storied history, the Altec Lansing brand has been known for responding to consumers' changing needs with innovative audio technology and hardware. The gaming industry is one of today's fastest growing markets where we have identified the need for quality and attainable products," said Ike S. Franco, CEO of Altec Lansing. "Our gaming range is a natural extension to our already existing product line, and we're excited to have Premier Accessory Group as our partner in this space."A sampling of the new line will be on display at CES 2020 in Central Hall, Booth #16029.
