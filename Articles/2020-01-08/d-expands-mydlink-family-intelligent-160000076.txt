The new mydlink cameras feature edge-based person detection with AI technology, allowing for more intelligent and personal automation and surveillance for every smart home. Not only are the cameras interoperable with other devices in the mydlink ecosystem, but they also work with the Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa, as well as conform to the ONVIF standard.
