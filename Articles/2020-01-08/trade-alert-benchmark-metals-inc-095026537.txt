A high insider ownership often makes company leadership more mindful of shareholder interests. Benchmark Metals insiders own about CA$7.4m worth of shares. That equates to 16% of the company. When combined with notable insider ownership, these factors suggest Benchmark Metals insiders are well aligned, and that they may think the share price is too low.
