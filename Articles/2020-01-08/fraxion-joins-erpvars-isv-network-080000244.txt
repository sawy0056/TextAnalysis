ERPVAR welcomes Fraxion, the only cloud-based procure-to-pay solution for business expense management serving the mid-market.ERPVAR's partnership with Fraxion focuses on promoting Fraxion's Acumatica and Sage 100 integrations. Fraxion is an Acumatica-Certified Application.In addition to Acumatica, Fraxion products integrate to leading Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions, including QuickBooks, Microsoft Dynamics GP, NAV, Business Central, SL, Sage 100, 200, Evo, 300, 500, SAP and Epicor.About Fraxion Fraxion's solutions take the risk out of procurement processes, proactively managing spend and ensuring that all expenditure is approved and within budget. The modular design covers purchasing, PO generation, expense, travel, supplier and invoice approval with Punchout, advanced analytics and budget insight. Their customers enjoy mobile flexibility, paperless simplicity and 100% policy compliance in user friendly solutions that guarantee savings.Fraxion is top-rated by Gartner and trusted by thousands of leading brands worldwide. The end-to-end solutions simplify spend management through ease and access to ensure your business spend is always under control. Fraxion's solutions are actively managing over $10 billion of spend across the globe with over 1,000 organizations and more than 200,000 users worldwide.For more information, visit https://www.fraxion.biz/ For press inquiries, email media@fraxion.bizAbout ERP VAR Established in 2012 and headquartered in Orange County, CA, ERPVAR represents the collective expertise of Sage 100cloud, Sage 300cloud, Sage Intacct, Microsoft Dynamics GP, NetSuite, Acumatica and QuickBooks Enterprise third-party developers combined with the expertise of local implementation ERP consultants. These local ERP consultants provide end-users with a thorough needs analysis to determine which ERP software functionality is required to address their unique needs. If a business process assessment is required, the local channel partner is enlisted to provide a comprehensive examination with end-user company departments to ensure the correct ERP software is selected, implemented and the staff is properly trained.
