The Apple-certified AL9111 3-in-1 Wireless Apple charging station and the AL9257 headphone charging stand with wireless charging pad are the first of their kind to be released by Altec Lansing. The AL9111 charging station allows for iPhone, Apple Watch and AirPods charging all in one compact unit while the AL9257 works with all wireless and Qi-enabled devices to offer dual charging.The new line is designed and manufactured through ESI Cellular, who has the exclusive license to Altec Lansing charging accessories in the U.S. and Canada. The licensing agreement includes charging cables, auxiliary cables, wall chargers, car chargers, power banks and charging pads."We are always looking to provide charging solutions to meet demands and offer an innovative product that keeps up with the lifestyle of consumers," says Ike S. Franco, CEO of Altec Lansing. "More and more products in today's landscape need to be charged daily and we are meeting the needs of consumers by streamlining the process with one collective solution. These products eliminate the need for multiple chargers and wires, making it easier to manage your devices."The AL9111 and AL9257 join a long line of wireless charging pads, cables and other accessories from Altec Lansing. Key features of the AL9111 and AL9257 include:AL9111 3-in-1 Wireless Apple Charging Station Designed as a charging hub for Apple Watch, AirPods, and iPhone Angled charging pad works with wireless and Qi-enabled devices 10W wireless fast charger AirPods charger MFI Apple certifiedAL9257 Headphone Charging Stand with Wireless Charging Pad Rubberized soft touch finish Headphone magnetic smart connection system Wireless 10W charge pad works with all wireless and Qi-enabled devices Wireless output: 10W Headphone charge output: 5V/1A Overcharge/overheat protection LED charging lightThe AL9111 and AL9257 are set to launch in Q1 2020, with price points of $79.99 and $29.99 respectively. MSRP for the product line will range from $9.99 to $99.99.
