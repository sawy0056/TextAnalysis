The complete, corrected release follows:Nationwide Mortgage Bankers, Inc. earns Great Place to Work certificationMELVILLE, N.Y., Jan. 7, 2020 /PRNewswire/ -- Independent mortgage lender Nationwide Mortgage Bankers, Inc. has achieved Great Place to Work certification in the 12-month period between Dec. 2019 to Dec. 2020. The methodology of this prestige comes from employee results to GPTW's Trust Index© Survey on the bases of credibility, respect, fairness, pride and camaraderie. This survey has a margin of error of only 5% or less, so it is considered to be a highly reputable resource."I am very excited to announce we have achieved our official Great Place to Work Certification," said Richard Steinberg, Founder & Chairman of the Board. "This is a very prestigious honor and puts us alongside some of the biggest companies in America. This achievement would not be possible without all of the hard work from each and every one of our talented staff."In just eight years of business, Nationwide Mortgage Bankers has received acclaim, landing on multiple lists. The most recent was a 51st place rank on Inc. 5000's Fastest-Growing Companies in the U.S. Though this ranking is primarily based on revenue growth, the number of employees working for NMB has grown substantially in 2019, particularly with the opening of its new office in Independence, Ohio.Nationwide Mortgage Bankers looks forward to fostering a stimulating and positive work environment in the new year. In 2020 and beyond, NMB will continue to create plenty of opportunities for collaboration and develop equitable workplace practices that ensure our employees are receiving equal opportunities for success.NMB is a private, independent mortgage lender with a customer-first focus that outpaces many of its competitors. The lender offers traditional fixed- and adjustable-rate mortgages at competitive rates.
