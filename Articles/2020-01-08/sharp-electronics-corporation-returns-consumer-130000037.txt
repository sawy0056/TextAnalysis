Led by smart kitchen products from the Sharp Home Electronics Company of America (SHCA), SEC will also exhibit its smart life solutions along with office and business products and 8K display products.(PRNewsfoto/Sharp Electronics Corporation)More"The entire Sharp team is thrilled to be back at CES to show all of the incredible products we've been working on over the past year," said Jim Sanduski, President, SHCA. "The last 12 months have been a time of tremendous innovation for us as we continued to leverage our cooking heritage, which spans more than half a century, in the development of modern, connected products that double down on our promise to deliver Simply Better Living to consumers."Sharp's entire kitchen suite of products will be on display at the Las Vegas Convention Center, including its Microwave Drawer™ Ovens, SuperSteam+™ Built-In Ovens, Over-the-Range Microwave Ovens, and its line of Plasmacluster® Ion Air Purifiers."We're taking Sharp – and the kitchen – to new levels with our kitchen suite of appliances," said Peter Weedfald, Senior Vice President of Sales and Marketing, Sharp Electronics Marketing Company of America. "Our goal with the development and introduction of every Sharp product is to service the imaginations of consumers. We remain committed and determined to fulfill the dreams of home cooks across the U.S.  We truly believe our current lineup of appliances, along with our newest introductions, remains the simplest to use and helps our consumers create great-tasting meals that support a healthier lifestyle."In addition, Sharp will be featuring several new products at CES, including:24 in. IoT Microwave Drawer™ Oven with Sharp Kitchen App & Easy Wave Open (SMD2489ES) 24 in. IoT SuperSteam+™ Built-in Wall Oven (SSC2489DS) 22.5 cu. ft. Counter Depth Refrigerator (SJG2351FS)  22 cu. ft. Counter Depth Refrigerator (SJG2254FS)  24 in. Dishwasher with Power Wash (SDW6757ES)  30 in. Stainless Steel Range Hood (SHC3051ES) and 36 in Stainless Steel Range Hood (SHC3651ES)  Rounding out Sharp's kitchen suite on display at CES is a line of Induction Cooktops and European Convection Wall Ovens, already introduced, which add a modern and elegant touch to any kitchen.In addition to its full range of home appliances, Sharp will also feature a number of its office and B2B products on display, including its Dynabook laptop lineup, see-through and reflective professional displays, 4K and 8K professional display products, and the Windows collaboration display from Sharp – the world's first with Microsoft's Skype for Business certification.Visit Sharp in booth 14006 at CES from January 7-10, 2020.About Sharp Electronics CorporationSharp Electronics Corporation is the U.S. subsidiary of Japan's Sharp Corporation.
