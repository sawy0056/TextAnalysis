This accomplishment demonstrates the maturity of our internal security processes which are vital to protecting our data. Our Information Security Management System is integrated with the T-Rex Management System; a foundation for our ISO 9001 certification.(PRNewsfoto/T-Rex Solutions)MoreMarc Steining, T-Rex Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer stated, "This achievement, when combined with our current ISO 9001:2015 certification and CMMI Level 3 for Development and Services ratings, validates that our practices are thorough, and any data system we manage is well protected. I'm confident in the data security work that we do and extremely proud of our IT and Security Team for reaching this goal."The International Organization for Standardization, often referred to as ISO, provides several management system standards that are certified by external bodies around the world. ISO/IEC 27001:2013 certification indicates T-Rex has established and implemented an information security management system.
