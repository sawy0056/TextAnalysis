It is a ridged belt used to harmonize the revolution of the crankshaft and the valves of the engine to facilitate the opening and the closing of the valves at a suitable interval. The timing belt is fixed beneath a timing cover located ahead of the engine. It is usually factory-made from the top quality rubber along with inside nylon covered strings to lengthen the life of the belt.
