The operator is licensed in multiple jurisdictions, and offers sports betting, in-play betting and online casinos, as well as being a leader in the growth market of eSports.The operator will initially use OtherLevels for desktop and mobile web engagement, integrated with their existing enterprise marketing platform. The initial contract is valued at approximately A$80,000.Brendan O'Kane the OtherLevels CEO commented, "We are very pleased to win another multi-country and multi-jurisdiction client, particularly one with a cutting edge focus on live in-play engagement, as well as the emerging area of e-sports. OtherLevels provides both OtherLevels Intelligent Messaging, an omni-channel messaging solution, as well as OtherLevels In-Play, a real-time live messaging solution for on-line sports betting and iGaming operators.
