The delisting takes place on Jan. 16.Accordingly, users will no longer be able to trade and deposit XRP, and they have until Feb. 16 to withdraw their funds.While Bitbox did not give specific reasons for the move, the exchange said it delists coins when they do not meet its standards based on “performance, reliability, liquidity, or law and regulatory requirements.”BitBox was launched in July 2018 and is one of the two cryptocurrency exchanges LINE operates to serve its over 80 million users. Japan-based Bitmax is the other exchange that recently received a license from the country’s financial regulator.
