like pink grapefruit juice, fresh pressed ginger, and hibiscus - that make our cocktails taste delicious," shared Ms. Bennett, "Plus, at 10% ABV, our refreshing cocktails are great for many occasions - from happy hour to weekend brunch." The ready-to-drink (RTD) cocktail category is on the rise, driven by increased consumer demand for convenience and better ingredients.  According to Nielsen data, the RTD spirit-based cocktail category grew 40.7% over the last year. Spirit-based RTD cocktail sales reached $62 million USD in 2018. In joining forces with our friends at Classic Wines, Trade Eights Cocktails will help satisfy this fast-growing consumer need in California.About Trade Eights CocktailsTrade Eights Cocktails (www.tradeeightscocktails.com) is an all-natural, bottled cocktail company based in Seattle and founded by entrepreneurs, Liane and Greg Bennett.  The company's craft cocktails have been recognized with multiple medals at the 2019 SIP Awards, the 2019 New York International Spirits Competition and the National Honey Board's 2019 National Honey Spirits Competition.  Committed to animal welfare causes, Trade Eights Cocktails gives 1% of every sale to organizations dedicated to the protection and well-being of animals.About Bronco Wine CompanyBronco Wine Company (www.broncowine.com) is one of the largest wine producers in the U.S. Founded in 1973 by Fred T., Joseph S. and John Franzia, the family-owned winery is committed to growing, producing and selling the finest quality wines of the highest value to their customers.
