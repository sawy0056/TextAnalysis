Its VA panel along with 5120x1440 resolution creates a more realistic gaming experience putting gamers in the middle of the action.Smooth and Speedy The new AOC AGON AG493UCX features a 1ms response time to grant a smear-free enhanced experience without the effects of ghosting during transitions. Its AMD Radeon FreeSync 2 technology provides smooth gaming and twice the frame rate of other monitors with a 120Hz refresh rate to eliminate image stuttering and blurry motion.Ergonomically Designed This beefy monitor is ergonomically designed. It harbors a height-adjustable stand with tilt and swivel abilities to improve posture and promote comfort. Further increase work efficiency with a Vesa wall mount to achieve sit-stand versatility or to reduce eye and neck strain helping users feel more comfortable at their desks.Re-Spawned 4-Year WarrantyThe AOC AGON AG493UCX is covered by AOC's latest Re-Spawned Warranty, which covers four years general warranty for any defect, including a one-time accidental warranty. That means gamers now have an opportunity to exchange their monitor for any reason. The new Re-Spawned Warranty applies to AGON and G Series models, including the popular AG273QCG, AG322QC4 and the AG271QX.     Industry-Leading Warranty up to 4-YearsAll premium AGON monitors are now covered by the AOC Re-Spawned Warranty for four years from purchase date. AOC Gaming G-Series monitors are covered for three years from purchase date. The coverage is retroactive to January 1, 2019.     Zero Dead Pixel GuaranteeIf even one pixel on your AGON or AOC Gaming monitor is dead or "bright," AOC will send you a new monitor.     Advance ReplacementAOC will send your replacement monitor right away – without waiting to receive your old monitor.     One-time Accidental Damage CoverageThis warranty covers all accidental damage and one incident is covered for up to one year.
