We moved in two years ago and have since doubled our square footage due to expanding shipments and employee additions. We are well prepared for future growth as we continue to add sales representatives across the country at a rapid rate." About Zynex, Inc. Zynex, founded in 1996, markets and sells its own design of electrotherapy medical devices used for pain management and rehabilitation; and the company's proprietary NeuroMove device designed to help recovery of stroke and spinal cord injury patients. Zynex is also developing a new blood volume monitor for use in hospitals and surgery centers.
