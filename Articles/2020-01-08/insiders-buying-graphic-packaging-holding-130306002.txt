So before you buy or sell Graphic Packaging Holding Company (NYSE:GPK), you may well want to know whether insiders have been buying or selling.What Is Insider Buying?It is perfectly legal for company insiders, including board members, to buy and sell stock in a company. However, rules govern insider transactions, and certain disclosures are required.We don't think shareholders should simply follow insider transactions. But it is perfectly logical to keep tabs on what insiders are doing. As Peter Lynch said, 'insiders might sell their shares for any number of reasons, but they buy them for only one: they think the price will rise.
