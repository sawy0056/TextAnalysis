Gross margin (non-GAAP) is expected to be within the lower half of the company's original guidance range of 48.5% to 50.5%.The Company will release its fourth quarter 2019 financial results after the market closes on February 6, 2020 and will host a conference call on February 7 at 8:00 a.m. Eastern Time.(Speakers)Wallace Kou, President & CEORiyadh Lai, CFO
