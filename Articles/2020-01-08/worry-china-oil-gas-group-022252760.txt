Check out our latest analysis for China Oil And Gas Group How Does Tie-liang Xu's Compensation Compare With Similar Sized Companies?Our data indicates that China Oil And Gas Group Limited is worth HK$1.5b, and total annual CEO compensation was reported as HK$7.3m for the year to December 2018. It is worth noting that the CEO compensation consists almost entirely of the salary, worth HK$7.2m. We examined companies with market caps from HK$778m to HK$3.1b, and discovered that the median CEO total compensation of that group was HK$2.3m.It would therefore appear that China Oil And Gas Group Limited pays Tie-liang Xu more than the median CEO remuneration at companies of a similar size, in the same market. However, this fact alone doesn't mean the remuneration is too high. It's good to see a bit of revenue growth, as this suggests the business is able to grow sustainably. Although we don't have analyst forecasts you might want to assess this data-rich visualization of earnings, revenue and cash flow.Has China Oil And Gas Group Limited Been A Good Investment?Since shareholders would have lost about 48% over three years, some China Oil And Gas Group Limited shareholders would surely be feeling negative emotions. It therefore might be upsetting for shareholders if the CEO were paid generously.In Summary...We compared total CEO remuneration at China Oil And Gas Group Limited with the amount paid at companies with a similar market capitalization. We found that it pays well over the median amount paid in the benchmark group.However, the earnings per share growth over three years is certainly impressive. Having said that, shareholders may be disappointed with the weak returns over the last three years. Considering the per share profit growth, but keeping in mind the weak returns, we'd need more time to form a view on CEO compensation.
