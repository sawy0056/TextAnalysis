VANCOUVER, BRITISH COLUMBIA--(Marketwired - May 13, 2014) -
GVIC Communications Corp. ("GVIC" or the "Company") (GCT.TO) announced today that the Board of Directors declared a quarterly dividend of $0.006 per share to shareholders of record on June 13, 2014 and payable on July 4, 2014.The dividend is consistent with the Company's dividend policy of paying $0.024 per share per annum payable quarterly.Shares in GVIC are traded on the Toronto Stock Exchange under the symbol GCT.About the Company: GVIC Communications Corp. is an information communications company focused on the provision of primary and essential information and related services through print, electronic and online media.
