While under CCAA protection, creditors and others are stayed from enforcing any rights against the Company, Lydian Canada, Lydian UK and a number of their direct subsidiaries including Lydian Armenia.All inquiries regarding the CCAA proceeding should be directed to the Monitor (email lydian@alvarezandmarsal.com or telephone +1 416-847-5158).
