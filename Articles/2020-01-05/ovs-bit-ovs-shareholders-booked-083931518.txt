View our latest analysis for OVS While the efficient markets hypothesis continues to be taught by some, it has been proven that markets are over-reactive dynamic systems, and investors are not always rational. It makes sense to check some of the other fundamental data for an explanation of the share price rise.OVS's revenue actually dropped 10% over last year.
