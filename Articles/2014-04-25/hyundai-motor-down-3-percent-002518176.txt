SEOUL (Reuters) - Shares in Hyundai Motor Co <005380.KS> dropped more than 3 percent on Friday after the automaker reported a smaller than expected quarterly profit hit by a strong Korean won.Shares in Hyundai, South Korea's second-biggest company, fell 2.7 percent as of 0005 GMT (1.05 a.m. BST), after falling as much as 3.3 percent to their lowest level in nearly one month.(Reporting by Hyunjoo Jin; Editing by Stephen Coates)
