TAIPEI, Jan 27 (Reuters) - Foxconn Technology Group, the major supplier of Apple Inc's iPhone 5 and iPads, sees its revenue surging to T$10 trillion ($333 billion) over the next decade from T$4 trillion now.Group chairman Terry Gou made the comment at a company event on Sunday.The group includes its flagship unit, Hon Hai Precision , and others such as Foxconn Technology Co.
