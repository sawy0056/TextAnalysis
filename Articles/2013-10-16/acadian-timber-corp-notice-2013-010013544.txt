To access this rebroadcast, please call 1-800-319-6413 or +1-604-638-9010 (code:2826).Acadian Timber Corp. is a leading supplier of primary forest products in Eastern Canada and the Northeastern U.S. With a total of 2.4 million acres of land under management, Acadian is the second largest timberland operator in New Brunswick and Maine.
