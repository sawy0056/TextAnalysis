Glenn Greenwald, the journalist who broke the news about the NSA spying scandal, is leaving the Guardian for what he describes as “a once-in-a-career dream journalistic opportunity that no journalist could possibly decline.” BuzzFeed broke that news Tuesday afternoon, but Greenwald wouldn’t say where exactly it is that he’s going.On Tuesday evening, however, Reuters cited two unidentified sources who said that the new media venture is backed by eBay founder Pierre Omidyar.
