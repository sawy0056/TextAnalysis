VANCOUVER, BRITISH COLUMBIA--(Marketwired - Jul 15, 2013) -
TAD Mineral Exploration Inc. ("TAD" or the "Company") (TSX VENTURE:TJ) wishes to announce it has acquired acreage prospective for hydrothermal graphite in Ontario. This new prospect, the 'Constance Lake' hydrothermal graphite prospect borders Zenyatta Ventures Ltd. Management plans to mobilize crews on this prospect shortly.Mr. Gregory Thomson, P.Geo., QP and a director of TAD states, "The geological setting of this region is one that similar formations seem to occur in pod like clusters, therefore it is probable that more one than one significant discovery such as the one Zenyatta has recently discovered, may also be discovered in the direct vicinity."This new prospect consists of 16 claims making up approximately 635 acres.
