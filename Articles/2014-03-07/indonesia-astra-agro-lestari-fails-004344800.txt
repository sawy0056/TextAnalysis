JAKARTA, March 6 (Reuters) - PT Astra Agro Lestari Tbk AALI.JK, Indonesia's largest listed plantation firm, failed to sell 6,000 tonnes of crude palm oil (CPO) at an auction on Wednesday due to low bids, the firm said in a statement.Astra Agro offered the CPO at between 10,605 and 10,770 rupiah per kg.($1 = 11,580 rupiah) (Compiled by Michael Taylor)
