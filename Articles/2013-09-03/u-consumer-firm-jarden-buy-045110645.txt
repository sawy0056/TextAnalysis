(Reuters) - U.S. consumer products company Jarden Corp (JAH) has agreed to buy scented candle maker Yankee Candle Company Inc (MDPRTC.UL) for $1.75 billion, the Wall Street Journal reported, citing people familiar with the matter.Jarden, whose products range from Sunbeam electric blankets and Crock-Pots to Coleman camping gear and Marmot clothing, is buying the company from private equity firm Madison Dearborn Partners LLC, which acquired Yankee Candle for $1.6 billion in 2006.Jarden is expected to fund the purchase with a combination of cash, debt and stock, the Journal said, adding that the deal is expected to be announced on Tuesday. (http://link.reuters.com/nus72v)Jarden and Madison Dearborn could not be reached for comment by Reuters outside of usual business hours.(Reporting by Krithika Krishnamurthy in Bangalore; Editing by Miral Fahmy)
