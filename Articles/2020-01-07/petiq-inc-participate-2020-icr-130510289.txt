(“PetIQ” or the “Company”) (PETQ), a leading pet medication and wellness company, today announced that the executive management team, including Cord Christensen, Chairman and CEO, and Susan Sholtis, President, will participate in the 2020 ICR Conference in Orlando, FL.The presentation will be held on Monday, January 13, 2020 at 11:00 a.m. ET and can be accessed live over the Internet hosted at the “Investors” section of the Company's website at www.PetIQ.com. About PetIQPetIQ is a leading pet medication and wellness company delivering a smarter way for pet parents to help their pets live their best lives through convenient access to affordable veterinary products and services.  The company engages with customers through more than 60,000 points of distribution across retail and e-commerce channels with its branded distributed medications, which is further supported by its own world-class medications manufacturing facility in Omaha, Nebraska.  The company’s national service platform, VIP Petcare, operates in over 3,400 retail partner locations in 41 states providing cost effective and convenient veterinary wellness services.  PetIQ believes that pets are an important part of the family and deserve the best products and care we can give them.  For more information, visit www.PetIQ.com.Contact: Investor Relations Contact: Media Relations Contact:
