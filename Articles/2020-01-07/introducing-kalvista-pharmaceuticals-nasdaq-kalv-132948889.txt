But in contrast you can make much more than 100% if the company does well. To wit, the KalVista Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (NASDAQ:KALV) share price has flown 123% in the last three years. Most would be happy with that. It's also good to see the share price up 67% over the last quarter. View our latest analysis for KalVista Pharmaceuticals Given that KalVista Pharmaceuticals didn't make a profit in the last twelve months, we'll focus on revenue growth to form a quick view of its business development. When a company doesn't make profits, we'd generally expect to see good revenue growth. Some companies are willing to postpone profitability to grow revenue faster, but in that case one does expect good top-line growth.KalVista Pharmaceuticals's revenue trended up 75% each year over three years. That's much better than most loss-making companies. Along the way, the share price gained 31% per year, a solid pop by our standards. But it does seem like the market is paying attention to strong revenue growth. That's not to say we think the share price is too high.
