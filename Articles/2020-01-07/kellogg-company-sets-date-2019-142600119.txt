Speaking on behalf of Kellogg Company will be Steve Cahillane, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer and Amit Banati, Chief Financial Officer.  A question and answer session with analysts and investors will follow.  A transcript of the conference call will be posted on the Company website within 24 hours of the event.
