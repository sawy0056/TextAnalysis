Attendees participate in relevant, interactive discussions on safety, claims, personnel, operations, and leadership.Speakers for the Long Beach event include:Woody Collins, Program Manager, AEU LEAD Shawn Galloway, President, ProAct Safety, Inc. Jason Lake, Loss Control Manager, The American Equity Underwriters, Inc. Jimmy Burgin, SVP, Director of Loss Control, The American Equity Underwriters, Inc. Will Scheffler, CPCU, AIC, SVP, Director of Claims, The American Equity Underwriters, Inc. Kayla Tortorich, SVP, Medical Management, The American Equity Underwriters, Inc.The event is free to members of the American Longshore Mutual Association (ALMA) and $350 for the public. More information and registration details are available on the event website.About The American Equity Underwriters, Inc.With headquarters in Mobile, Alabama, The American Equity Underwriters, Inc. serves as program administrator for American Longshore Mutual Association Ltd., a group self-insurance fund authorized by the U.S. Department of Labor for waterfront employers in all 50 states under the United States Longshore and Harbor Workers' Compensation Act. AEU provides best-in-class services including claims handling, loss control, longshore consulting and other support. For more information, call (866) 238-8754 or visit amequity.com.
