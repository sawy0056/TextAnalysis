What can be done to prevent getting trapped into this cynical, negative cycle?Think great thoughts. A small group of eleven Christian men based in the Twin Cities, they call themselves the SAM Group (for Saturday morning), have met with one another each week for ten years at Christ Presbyterian Church in Edina, MN and have collectively authored and released a new book titled Good Men Great Thoughts: A Daily Devotional.  This new book, released December 16, 2019, has reached international bestseller status across eight categories in the US, Canada and Australia on Amazon. Co-author Frank Pleticha said, "No one was born to survive and languish. Everyone is born to thrive and flourish. Born to be great. Great people fill their minds with great thoughts.  There's no better way to start the day right than by reading and applying a great thought.  To stay focused, optimistic and productive."In this bestselling book, discover the great thoughts - organized in a daily devotional - the SAM Group brothers have shared with one another.  Drawn from Christian authors, the Bible, leaders and prominent people in the secular world, these great thoughts can help in all areas of life and lead readers to become the best people they are intended to be.   Co-author Dave Hemink stated, "Within the book, they share the story of their vibrant small group, the purpose of which is to encourage one another, live adventurously, grow in their faith and become better men. A short biography of each member, along with many group pictures, allows the reader to get to know the men behind the great thoughts, and to share in their journey.""This book is a must have for those who value daily inspiration and a daily call to action in 2020 to become their best," commented co-author Pat Siebenaler.Good Men Great Thoughts: A Daily Devotional is available in both paperback and Kindle formats at:  Good Men Great Thoughts: A Daily DevotionalOne hundred percent of the royalties from the book go to two SAM Group supported charities: Wings for Widows and Mobility Worldwide-Minnesota.  Contact information:
