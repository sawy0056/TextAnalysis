Hinkle details the impact of past life regression sessions on her spiritual journey in 'New Perspectives, Past Lives: An Exploration in Healing'SAFETY HARBOR, Fla., Jan. 6, 2020 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- After holistic medicine practitioner and author Shari A. Hinkle connected with mutual friend Patricia McGivern, a nationally certified hypnotist who specializes in past life regression, she decided to take the leap and unpack baggage in her life to find true spiritual healing. Her story and the incredible experiences she unlocked is detailed in her moving new memoir, "New Perspectives, Past Lives: An Exploration in Healing," a look at how regression sessions peeled back the layers of her current life to reveal those she lived centuries ago and how these newfound memories were intertwined with the present.Hinkle's incredible two-year journey exploring reincarnation and expanding her concept of energy deepened her view of spiritual healing and led to unlikely discoveries, such as regaining her lost sense of smell. A story like no other, Hinkle's honest approach toward hypnotism and past life regression invites readers to understand the importance of a connection to higher powers and gain awareness of all the ways the universe speaks to us.By taking readers on a journey to the past, Hinkle emphasizes the relevance these experiences have on the present and the lessons she took away from each session."I wanted to express that there are other ways of healing beyond western medicine," Hinkle says. "We all have the power to heal ourselves.""New Perspectives, Past Lives" is the perfect read for those eager to discover how an open-minded and curious woman found all the answers for healing within herself. Readers will certainly enjoy Hinkle's exploratory mission toward gaining newfound respect for the past and renewed optimism for the future."New Perspectives, Past Lives: An Exploration in Healing" By Shari A. Hinkle ISBN: 9781982233457 (softcover); 9781982233471 (hardcover); 9781982233464 (electronic) Available at the Balboa Press Online Bookstore, Amazon and Barnes & NobleAbout the author Shari Hinkle is a devoted practitioner in holistic medicine and spiritual healing. A certified hypnotist, Pilates instructor and massage therapist, in 2000 she opened New Perspectives Body Care & Pilates Studio in Palm Harbor, Florida and has been helping others maintain a healthy lifestyle through massage, skincare and Pilates ever since. Hinkle resides in Safety Harbor and is a mother to two adult sons. "New Perspectives, Past Lives" is her first book.
