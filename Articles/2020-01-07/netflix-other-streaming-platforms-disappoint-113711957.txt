production subsidiary Amazon Studios managed three wins, all for the TV show "Fleabag."Fleabag won the award for the best TV series in the musical or comedy genre. Phoebe Waller-Bridge won for the best actress in the genre and Andrew Scott won for best actor in a supporting role.The Walt Disney Company's (NYSE: DIS) Hulu also bagged two awards.Patricia Arquette won an award for best actress in a supporting role for "The Act," and Ramy Youssef won for best performance by an actor in a music order comedy for the tv series "Ramy."Apple Inc.'s
