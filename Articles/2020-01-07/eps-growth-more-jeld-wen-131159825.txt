See our latest analysis for JELD-WEN Holding How Quickly Is JELD-WEN Holding Increasing Earnings Per Share?If a company can keep growing earnings per share (EPS) long enough, its share price will eventually follow. That means EPS growth is considered a real positive by most successful long-term investors. Who among us would not applaud JELD-WEN Holding's stratospheric annual EPS growth of 56%, compound, over the last three years? While that sort of growth rate isn't sustainable for long, it certainly catches my attention; like a crow with a sparkly stone.I like to take a look at earnings before interest and (EBIT) tax margins, as well as revenue growth, to get another take on the quality of the company's growth. JELD-WEN Holding reported flat revenue and EBIT margins over the last year. That's not bad, but it doesn't point to ongoing future growth, either.In the chart below, you can see how the company has grown earnings, and revenue, over time. As a result, I'm encouraged by the fact that insiders own JELD-WEN Holding shares worth a considerable sum. Indeed, they hold US$26m worth of its stock. That shows significant buy-in, and may indicate conviction in the business strategy. Even though that's only about 1.1% of the company, it's enough money to indicate alignment between the leaders of the business and ordinary shareholders.Is JELD-WEN Holding Worth Keeping An Eye On?JELD-WEN Holding's earnings per share growth have been levitating higher, like a mountain goat scaling the Alps. That EPS growth certainly has my attention, and the large insider ownership only serves to further stoke my interest. At times fast EPS growth is a sign the business has reached an inflection point; and I do like those. So yes, on this short analysis I do think it's worth considering JELD-WEN Holding for a spot on your watchlist.
