On display in booth 3837 will be APG's extensive portfolio of global cash drawer offerings, POS partners and the latest addition to the SMARTtill® Solution Suite: CurrentSee® Software, providing real-time cash visibility at the POS."We are honored to be selected for the Future & In-store Technology category on the NRF 2020 Expo tour," stated Stephen Bergeron, V.P. of Global Sales and Marketing North America. "As retailers face rising labor rates and difficulties finding a lack of qualified labor, we understand the challenges many face today. Retailers that choose to leverage automated cash management solutions and future in-store technology will reduce shrink, reduce labor costs, and improve the overall customer experience," continued Bergeron.As the retail POS market evolves, the demands being placed on POS peripherals have changed," stated Bob Stone, Global V.P. of Product at APG. "POS life expectancy and cash volume differ by retail environment and pain point, requiring a tiered group of POS product offerings. From entry-level to robust, to mobile solutions, to fully custom and bespoke cash drawer solutions, our wide range of products is unique in the market allowing us to address a retailer's specification, environment and currency requirements."At NRF, APG will feature industry-leading partners Appetize, HP, NCR, Shopkeep, Square and Oracle in their booth. Partnering with leading point-of-sale systems providers, allows show attendees to see how the functionality and performance of these solutions vary for retail, hospitality, grocery and convenience stores verticals."Each year the NRF Show connects us with new and existing customers, partners and innovators from around the globe – these conversations serve as a catalyst to redefine and challenge our team to develop solutions to continually enhance efficiency and security at the point of sale," stated Bergeron.APG invites all industry partners, retail customers, friends, and attendees to visit them at NRF in booth 3837 to discover their full range of cash management solutions.Schedule a meeting with us at NRF 2020 to see the latest innovations in cash management technology.About APG Cash Drawer – APG Cash Drawer, with over 40 years of experience, manufactures a wide range of highly durable and reliable cash drawers that are delivered quickly to the marketplace. APG has built a reputation as the supplier of choice for cash management solutions for retail, grocery, hospitality, and quick-serve for thousands of customers throughout the world. Whether it's our general application cash drawer, custom-designed solutions, or the SMARTtill® Cash Management Solution, our products and brand are differentiated by our ability to deliver innovative technologies that globally enhance efficiency and security at the point of sale.
