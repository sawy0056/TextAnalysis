See our latest analysis for Build King Holdings How Does Derek Zen's Compensation Compare With Similar Sized Companies?According to our data, Build King Holdings Limited has a market capitalization of HK$1.1b, and paid its CEO total annual compensation worth HK$18m over the year to December 2018. It is worth noting that the CEO compensation consists almost entirely of the salary, worth HK$18m. We looked at a group of companies with market capitalizations under HK$1.6b, and the median CEO total compensation was HK$1.8m.It would therefore appear that Build King Holdings Limited pays Derek Zen more than the median CEO remuneration at companies of a similar size, in the same market. However, this fact alone doesn't mean the remuneration is too high.
