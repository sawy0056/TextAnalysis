(LHS.CN) (OTCQX: LHSIF)www.libertyhealthsciences.com ("Liberty" or the "Company"), a provider of high quality cannabis, announced today the appointment of Zeifmans LLP as the Company's new independent registered public accounting firm.View photosLHS Logo (CNW Group/Liberty Health Sciences Inc.)MoreZeifmans replaced MNP LLP (the "Former Auditor") as the Company's independent auditor to provide audit services for the Company effective December 18, 2019 . The appointment of Zeifmans has been approved by the directors of Liberty.There were no disagreements or unresolved issues with the Former Auditor on any matter of audit scope or procedures, accounting principles or policies, or financial statement disclosure.About Liberty Health Sciences Inc.Liberty is the cannabis provider committed to providing a trusted, high quality cannabis experience based on our genuine care for all cannabis users and a focus on operational excellence from seed to sale. Liberty's measured approach to expansion opportunities is focused on maximizing returns to shareholders, while keeping consumers' well-being at the forefront of what we do.
