Hand comes to the Warriors with nearly 20 years of volleyball coaching experience under her belt. Prior to arriving at Webber International University, Hand spent 2 years coaching at Wilson College working as the women's indoor and beach volleyball coach and the assistant coach for the men's volleyball team. Her enthusiasm and dedication to the sport are what inspired the idea of having more camp options for both boys and girls at Webber."Hand is an excellent addition to the Nike Volleyball Camps team," says Siera Love, VP of Nike Volleyball Camps. "Her passion to bring more opportunities to athletes is what makes us sure that these camps will be great for players at all levels."Starting in June, Hand will be coaching the Boys Intermediate and Advanced Indoor Volleyball Camps. Here, players ages 12-18 looking to work at a more advanced level can come train with a collegiate coaching staff in a competitive environment. In July, both girls and boys can expect Beach Volleyball Camps that are geared towards all ability levels. Training will be more fundamental focused and will teach campers how to play doubles on a smaller court.All camp programs will offer an overnight and extended day/commuter options for players to choose from, giving girls and boys the opportunity to experience the campus college life.Players, coaches, and parents interested in camps can visit Nike Volleyball Camps at http://www.ussportscamps.com/volleyball or call 1-800-645-3226.About US Sports CampsUS Sports Camps (USSC), headquartered in San Rafael, California, is America's largest sports camp network and the licensed operator of Nike Sports Camps.
