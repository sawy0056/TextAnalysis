About First Financial BancorpFirst Financial Bancorp is a Cincinnati, Ohio based bank holding company. As of September 30, 2019, the Company had $14.5 billion in assets, $9.1 billion in loans, $10.1 billion in deposits and $2.3 billion in shareholders' equity. The Company's subsidiary, First Financial Bank, founded in 1863, provides banking and financial services products through its six lines of business: Commercial, Retail Banking, Investment Commercial Real Estate, Mortgage Banking, Commercial Finance and Wealth Management. These business units provide traditional banking services to business and retail clients. Wealth Management provides wealth planning, portfolio management, trust and estate, brokerage and retirement plan services and had approximately $2.9 billion in assets under management as of September 30, 2019. The Company operated 145 full service banking centers as of September 30, 2019, primarily in Ohio, Indiana and Kentucky, while the Commercial Finance business lends into targeted industry verticals on a nationwide basis.
