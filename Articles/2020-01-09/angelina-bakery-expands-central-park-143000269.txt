Thanks to this approach, it was after the great success and positive reception of their original Midtown location which opened in April, that the team decided to pursue an expansion.The menu options include their famous New York Times featured pillowy bomboloni doughnuts filled with an array of flavors, from the classics such as Nutella and cream, to the more sophisticated pistachio and green tea.
