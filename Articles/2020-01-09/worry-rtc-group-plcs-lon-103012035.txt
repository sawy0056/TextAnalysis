While this analysis focuses on total compensation, it's worth noting the salary is lower, valued at UK£260k. We looked at a group of companies with market capitalizations under UK£152m, and the median CEO total compensation was UK£252k.As you can see, Andy Pendlebury is paid more than the median CEO pay at companies of a similar size, in the same market. However, this does not necessarily mean RTC Group plc is paying too much.
