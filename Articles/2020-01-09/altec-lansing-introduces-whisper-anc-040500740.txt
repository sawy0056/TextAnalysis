Highlighted by the ComfortQ+, these headphones set themselves apart from others in the line with impressive quick charge technology which provides three hours of battery life for every five minutes of charging. The ComfortQ and ComfortQ+ also include key features such as the foldable on-the-go design, CoolTeQ ear cushions to keep you cool and comfortable, and a battery life of 26 hours.The ANC line of headphones includes the Whisper ANC, ComfortQ and ComfortQ+ which all feature "Just Ask" technology, a wireless range of up to 30 ft and boast from 14 to 26 hours of battery life."We strive to provide a diverse range of products to meet the ever-changing needs and expectations of our consumers," said Joe Campos, VP Sales and Marketing at Altec Lansing. "Our current line of active noise cancelling hea- dphones provides a range of options to fit your needs and budget.
