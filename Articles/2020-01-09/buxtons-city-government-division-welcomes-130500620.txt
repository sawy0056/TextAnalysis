The division supports local government agencies in making informed economic development decisions in areas ranging from retail recruitment to tourism through a suite of customer analytics-based products."The tremendous growth in our city government vertical is a testament to the power of our products and Buxton's world-class customer support," said Chris Briggs, senior vice president of Buxton's city government division. "We look forward to working with each of these agencies to help them reach their economic development goals."Agencies joining Buxton's client roster include Bellevue, Neb.; Bridgewater, Mass., the Brownfield Industrial Development Corporation of Brownfield, Texas; Hickory, N.C.; Kingsport, Tenn.; Littlefield, Texas; the New Bedford Economic Development Council of New Bedford, Mass.; Osage Beach, Mo.; Palm Springs, Calif.; Park Development LLC; Port Angeles, Wash.; Rankin, Texas; SeaTac, Wash.; the Stephenville Economic Development Authority of Stephenville, Texas; Trinidad, Colo.; and the Wagoner County Economic Development Authority of Wagoner County, Okla.Buxton has worked with hundreds of government agencies across the country to integrate data into economic development strategies.
