But if you pick the right stock, you can make a lot more than 100%. For example, the JonDeTech Sensors AB (publ) (STO:JDT) share price has soared 189% in the last year. Most would be very happy with that, especially in just one year! It's also good to see the share price up 65% over the last quarter. We'll need to follow JonDeTech Sensors for a while to get a better sense of its share price trend, since it hasn't been listed for particularly long. See our latest analysis for JonDeTech Sensors Given that JonDeTech Sensors didn't make a profit in the last twelve months, we'll focus on revenue growth to form a quick view of its business development. Generally speaking, companies without profits are expected to grow revenue every year, and at a good clip. As you can imagine, fast revenue growth, when maintained, often leads to fast profit growth.Over the last twelve months, JonDeTech Sensors's revenue grew by 33%. That's a fairly respectable growth rate. While that revenue growth is pretty good the share price performance outshone it, with a lift of 189% as mentioned above. Given that the business has made good progress on the top line, it would be worth taking a look at its path to profitability.
