One way to examine how market sentiment has changed over time is to look at the interaction between a company's share price and its earnings per share (EPS).During three years of share price growth, Aeffe achieved compound earnings per share growth of 42% per year. This EPS growth is higher than the 19% average annual increase in the share price.
