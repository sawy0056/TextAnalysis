The U.S. pharmaceutical company announced early Friday a deal to buy medical dermatology company Dermira in a deal valued at $1.1 billion. Under terms of the deal, Lilly will pay $18.75 in cash for each Dermira (NASDAQ:DERM) share outstanding, a 2.2% premium to Thursday's closing price of $18.34.
