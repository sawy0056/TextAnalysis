It is worth noting that the CEO compensation consists almost entirely of the salary, worth HK$1.9m. We took a group of companies with market capitalizations below HK$1.6b, and calculated the median CEO total compensation to be HK$1.8m.That means Wei Feng Zhou receives fairly typical remuneration for the CEO of a company that size. So given this relatively weak performance, shareholders would probably not want to see high compensation for the CEO.
