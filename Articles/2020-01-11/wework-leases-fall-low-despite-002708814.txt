CarterFox BusinessJanuary 10, 2020ReblogShareTweetShareWeWork is still working, but not as it once was.The embattled shared workspace provider scaled back the number of buildings it leased in New York and London during the last quarter of 2019, data from commercial-property company CoStar showed. In Manhattan, it leased 64,000 square feet of space, the lowest amount in five years. And in London, it leased 49,000 square feet, the lowest since 2016.But the slowdown in leases followed an uptick ahead of the company’s planned initial public offering, which has since been abandoned.
