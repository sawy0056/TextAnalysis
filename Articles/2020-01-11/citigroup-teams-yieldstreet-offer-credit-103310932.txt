It seeks to enable individual investors to access investments across a range of asset classes such as Real Estate, Marine Finance, Art Finance, Legal Finance and Commercial loans.Citi’s Spread Product Investment Technologies (Sprint) unit, created in 2018, is backing the partnership. It seeks to tie up with firms that aim to reshape the $40 trillion debt and private credit markets.“We have always regarded YieldStreet as an innovative platform in the market, bringing institutional-style investments to a broad audience,” said Matt Zhang, Head, Sprint, Citi.
