View our latest analysis for Logitech International How Does Bracken Darrell's Compensation Compare With Similar Sized Companies?Our data indicates that Logitech International S.A. is worth CHF7.8b, and total annual CEO compensation was reported as US$8.4m for the year to March 2019. We think total compensation is more important but we note that the CEO salary is lower, at US$925k. We note that more than half of the total compensation is not the salary; and performance requirements may apply to this non-salary portion. When we examined a selection of companies with market caps ranging from US$4.0b to US$12b, we found the median CEO total compensation was US$2.1m.Thus we can conclude that Bracken Darrell receives more in total compensation than the median of a group of companies in the same market, and of similar size to Logitech International S.A.. However, this doesn't necessarily mean the pay is too high.
