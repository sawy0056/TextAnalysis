Leong will be responsible for driving the expansion of VisasQ's business and operations in the Asia Pacific region.Since VQ was first launched in 2013, VisasQ has been constantly developing its global capabilities to cater for the growing demand of high quality subject matter experts in all industries and geographies. This has significantly increased the value and impact brought to clients ranging from professional service firms, institutional investors to industry corporates. VisasQ currently has over 10,000 advisors residing outside Japan, spanning every major economy and country."The opening of the Singapore office is a very important milestone for VisasQ as we seek to establish ourselves as a preeminent global player in the industry. We will continue to build on our strengths and capabilities to ensure that we keep offering our best to our clients," said Eiko Hashiba, VisasQ's CEO. "We are very excited to have Leong on board as he brings significant breadth and depth of industry experience to the VisasQ team, and we look forward to working with him to grow VisasQ's global business and create even greater value for our clients, company and employees.""Singapore is well known for its highly skilled and diverse professional talent pool, as well as excellent business infrastructure," said Leong. "By setting up its first international office in Singapore, VisasQ will have a robust base of international operations outside Japan to cover both client servicing and advisor acquisition. It will also be the ideal springboard for VisasQ to expand its servicing capabilities across Asia and beyond, and most importantly, deepens VisasQ's commitment to delivering superior service to every one of its clients."About VisasQ Singapore officeRepresentative: Leong Loh, Head of Singapore Office / Director of Asia Operations Address: 9 Straits View, Marina One West Tower, #05-07, Singapore 018937 URL: https://visasq.co.jp/en/ VisasQ Inc.
