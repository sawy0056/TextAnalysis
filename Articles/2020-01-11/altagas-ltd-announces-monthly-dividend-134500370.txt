Announces Monthly DividendCNW GroupJanuary 10, 2020ReblogShareTweetShareCALGARY , Jan. 10, 2020 /CNW/ - AltaGas Ltd. ("AltaGas") (ALA.TO) announced today that the January dividend will be paid on February 17, 2020 , to common shareholders of record on January 27, 2020 . The ex-dividend date is January 24, 2020 . The amount of the dividend will be $0.08 for each common share. This dividend is an eligible dividend for Canadian income tax purposes.As previously announced, AltaGas has suspended its Premium Dividend™, Dividend Reinvestment and Optional Cash Purchase Plan (the "Plan") indefinitely. The January dividend will not be eligible for reinvestment under the Plan.AltaGas is a leading North American energy infrastructure company with a focus on regulated Utilities, Midstream and Power. AltaGas creates value by growing and optimizing its energy infrastructure, including a focus on clean energy sources.
