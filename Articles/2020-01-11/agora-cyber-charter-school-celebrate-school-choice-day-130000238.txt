"Socialization is a key component of cyber education and School Choice Week presents a great opportunity to gather together and celebrate learning."This event is one of three hosted by Agora Cyber Charter School during National School Choice Week. Celebrations will also be held in Pittsburgh and Wilkes Barre. Agora Cyber Charter School, established in 2005, is a free online public school for Pennsylvania students in grades K-12. Agora's engaging curriculum is offered by dedicated, Pennsylvania-certified teachers who are committed to an individualized learning approach.As a nonpartisan, nonpolitical public awareness effort, National School Choice Week shines a positive spotlight on effective education options for students, families, and communities around the country. From January 26 through February 1, 2020, more than 50,000 independently-planned events will be held in celebration of the Week.
